Biography

Linghao Song is a postdoctoral researcher in UCLA Computer Science Department. He received Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Duke University in 2020, M.S. in Electrical Engineering from University of Pittsburgh in 2017, and B.S.E. in Information Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2014. His research interests include machine learning acceleration, computer architecture, FPGA accelerators, and deep learning application. He received 2020 EDAA Outstanding Dissertations Award and 2021 Duke ECE Outstanding Disseration Award.
